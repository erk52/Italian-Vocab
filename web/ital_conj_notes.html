The present tense uses a set of personal endings applied to the stem of the verb. These endings are mostly the same for each of the three verb conjugations, except for slight differences in the third person forms. You may notice that the third verb conjugation has two different forms. There is no clear rule to follow to know which category a particular verb belongs to. This is just something to learn and remember.
